Christopher Edward Nolan



About Nolan

  • Full Name: Christopher Edward Nolan
  • Birth: July 30, 1970, in London, England
  • Profession: Film director, screenwriter, producer
  • Early Work: Made his directorial debut with the low-budget film "Following" (1998)
  • Breakthrough: Gained wide recognition with "Memento" (2000), known for its unique narrative structure
  • Style: Non-linear narratives, themes of time and identity, practical effects
  • Frequent Collaborators: Hans Zimmer (composer), Wally Pfister (cinematographer)
  • Recognition: Academy award winner for Best Director and many others
